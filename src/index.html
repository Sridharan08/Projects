Adobe XD is a desktop program made for designing user interfaces and wireframes for a variety of applications. This collaborative tool allows BU educators and students to work together or alone on UI/UX projects, building designs for apps, websites, and much more.

Web Development

Adobe XD is a powerful and versatile tool for web designers. It offers a wide range of features for creating responsive websites that look great and perform well on all devices. XD. also makes prototyping designs easy, so you can test different ideas before committing to a final design.

Mobile App Development

Adobe XD makes it simple to create a mobile app. Webkul is a conversion method that allows you a variety of options for creating the best mobile app from Adobe-XD. Webkul gives primary tools, features, and the most common shortcuts to boost your Mobile app productivity.

Design

Adobe XD is the Adobe prototyping tool for user experience and interaction designers. Adobe XD features are used for creating wireframes, prototypes, and screen designs for digital products such as websites and mobile apps.
